01 · About

Young enough to ask basic questions. Serious enough to keep following them.

I am still at the beginning, and I do not want to hide that behind impressive words. Most of what I know has come from curiosity: opening a project, changing something, breaking it, searching for the reason, and trying again until the result finally makes sense.

What keeps me interested is the part people rarely see. A website is not only its colours. An application is not only its buttons. Behind every useful digital product there are decisions, logic, mistakes, revisions and many small details that have to work together.

I want to become good at that hidden work: understanding systems, explaining difficult ideas clearly, and building things that feel considered rather than rushed.

Developer community

Beyond the Code

Beyond the Code is built around a simple belief: good developers do more than make software run. They question assumptions, help one another, learn from failure and stay with hard problems long enough to understand them.

The name is not a promise that everything is possible overnight. It is a reminder that the most interesting work often begins after the obvious solution ends.

We do not stop at what the code does. We ask what it could become.
03 · In development

Beyond the Code · Experimental AI project

Something new is being built.

Beyond the Code, led by Anes Bensaibi, is currently developing a new website centred on experimental artificial intelligence. The project is being shaped as more than a public page: it is intended to become a working environment where advanced tools, original interaction ideas and carefully designed AI systems can be tested together.

At the centre of the project is a new language model that is still in its early development stage. The team is exploring how a model can become more capable, more useful and easier to control without simply copying the behaviour of existing assistants. The work includes research into reasoning, file understanding, interface design, safety and the way people communicate with intelligent systems.

The ambition is serious, but so is the responsibility. No unfinished feature will be presented as a breakthrough before it has been tested. Technical details, demonstrations and public access will be shared gradually as the project becomes stable enough to deserve them.

Project status
Private development — experimental AI systems and a new language model are currently being researched, designed and tested.
Not a finished product. Not an empty promise. A difficult idea being turned into real work, one tested step at a time.
